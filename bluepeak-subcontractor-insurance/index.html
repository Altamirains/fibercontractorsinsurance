What insurance do I need to work as a Bluepeak subcontractor?

Bluepeak typically requires: General Liability ($1M/$2M), statutory Workers' Compensation, Commercial Auto ($1M CSL), and possibly an Umbrella policy for larger projects. Bluepeak must be named as Additional Insured on GL and Auto policies. We issue compliant COIs same-day.

Is Bluepeak the same as Midcontinent Communications?

Bluepeak was created from Midcontinent Communications' fiber operations and operates in similar Great Plains markets. While related in heritage, Bluepeak is now an independent brand focused on competitive gigabit fiber. COIs should name Bluepeak, not Midcontinent — we know the correct entity names.
