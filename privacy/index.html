Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request through this site, we collect information you provide directly, including your name, company name, email address, phone number, state, trade type, annual revenue range, and coverage needs.

We also collect analytics data through Google Analytics, including pages visited, time spent on the site, browser and device type, and general geographic location. This data is collected in aggregate and is used to improve the site experience.

2How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used to connect you with licensed insurance brokers who can provide quotes tailored to your business. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with the licensed brokers and carriers working directly to fulfill your quote request on your behalf.

3Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (tracking ID: G-VRWZREBP56) to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics may collect anonymized data including your IP address, browser type, device information, and the pages you visit. This information is used solely to improve site performance and content.

You can opt out of Google Analytics data collection at any time by using Google's opt-out browser add-on or by adjusting your browser's privacy settings.

4Cookies

This site may use cookies to support analytics functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or remarketing purposes. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features but will not prevent you from submitting a quote request.

5Data Retention

Quote request data is retained for the purpose of fulfilling your insurance quote request and for legal and compliance record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us through the quote form on this site. We will respond to verified deletion requests promptly.

6Your Rights

Depending on the state in which you reside, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the quote request form on this site.

Our site may contain links to third-party websites, including insurance carrier sites, ISP contractor portals, and industry resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8Children's Privacy

This website is intended for use by business owners and contractors and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10Contact

For privacy-related questions, data access requests, or deletion requests, please contact us through the quote request form on this site. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns in a timely and transparent manner.
